Welcome to Grid Duel!

Grid Duel is a variant of the classic Three Men Morries strategy game where you aim to form a connecting row of three of your own pieces while preventing your opponent from doing the same.

Game Rules

  • At the beginning, each player's 3 pieces are placed at opposite ends of the board.
  • Players take turns moving one of their pieces to an adjacent empty spot.
  • Form a connecting row of three pieces to win the game.
  • Prevent your opponent from forming a connecting row while trying to create your own.
